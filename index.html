About NAVAK

About The Event

Navak is a Hackathon conducted by IEDC ICET.The hackathon will be based on a socially relevent theme. Prior programming experience is not required for this hackathon.This event will be 24 hour marathon event where candidates can crack given tasks and be the winners.

Navak is open to all skillsets and ideas.We believe that talent from all walks of life makes our community uniquie and helps to develop more comprehensive solutions.

Venue

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology

Time

Thursday to Friday
2-3 December 2021

Rules & Regulations
  • Candidates should participate as a team of four.
  • Each team should carry their own internet and other resources such as extension cords etc.
  • Each team should carry laptops.
  • All participants should follow Covid Protocols.
  • Only ten of the competing teams will present their projects in the final round of judging at the end of the event.
  • Simulation should be done in recomended software(Proteus).
  • Its better to have the recomended skills:
    • Googling
    • Problem Solving
    • Presentation
    • MS Office
    • Infrastructure design
    • Estimation
    • CAD
    • Simulation in Proteus
NB: Its not necessary to all skills, help will be provided.
